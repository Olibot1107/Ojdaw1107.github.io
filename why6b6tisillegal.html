False Advertising on 6b6t: A Legal Issue?

In a recent video (YouTube link), a 6b6t player claims that lag machines don’t work. However, this statement is false—lag machines do work, meaning this claim could be considered false advertising.

Why is This Illegal?

False advertising is illegal in many jurisdictions, whether the service is free or paid. Here are some key legal references:

  • General False Advertising Laws - According to Bing’s search results, false advertising is illegal, regardless of whether the service is free or paid.
  • Polish Advertising Law - A legal firm confirms that false advertising is illegal in Poland, whether or not money is involved (source).
  • Polish Government Confirmation - The Polish government explicitly states that false advertising is illegal, whether free or paid (official site).

Additionally, since 6b6t sells ranks, this means it falls into the category of paid services, making the false claim even more problematic.
